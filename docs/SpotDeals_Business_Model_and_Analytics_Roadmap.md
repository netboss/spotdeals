# SpotDeals Business Model & Analytics Roadmap

## Principle

Monetize ownership, automation and insights---not public information.

## SpotDeals Insights

### Phase 1

-   Search appearances
-   Venue views
-   Deal views
-   Website clicks
-   Directions clicks
-   Phone clicks
-   CTA clicks
-   Daily/weekly trends
-   Deal performance
-   Top search terms
-   Discovery sources

### Phase 2

-   Month-over-month trends
-   Best performing deal
-   Best performing day

### Phase 3

-   Benchmarking
-   AI recommendations

### Phase 4

-   Weekly email reports

## Technical Architecture

Capture first-party events: - timestamp - venue_nid - deal_nid -
event_type - session_id - search_query - referrer - device

Aggregate into reporting tables. Do not store counters directly on
Drupal nodes.

## Monetization

Premium features: - Claimed listings - Owner editing - Instant
publishing - Analytics - Featured placement - AI assistance -
Promotions - Push notifications - Rich media

## Current Recommendation

-   Keep first featured deal free.
-   Allow editors to approve additional verified public deals.
-   Charge for business tools, ownership and insights instead of
    restricting public deals.
